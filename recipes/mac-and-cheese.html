Mac and Cheese

Here we will be making the tastiest Mac and Cheese you ever put in your mouth. With this recipe, we will give you the instructions and ingredients you will need. This recipe is pretty quick about 30 minutes and yield 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • Ground black pepper to taste
