Mac and Cheese

Here we will be making the tastiest Mac and Cheese you ever put in your mouth. With this recipe, we will give you the instructions and ingredients you will need. This recipe is pretty quick about 30 minutes and yield 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Steps

Follow the steps below to cook your tasty mac and cheese.

  1. Boil the Macaroni

    2. Start boiling water in a pot and cook the elbow Macaroni for 8 minutes.

  2. Make a Roux

    3. Melt butter in pan with medium heat. When all the butter as melted, add salt, flour, and pepper and stir until smooth. Slowly, pour your milk and stir until you see the mixture is smooth and bubbling on low heat. Careful to not let the Roux burn.

  3. Add the Cheese and Macaroni

    4. Take your shredded cheese and add it to the Roux. Stir the cheese slowly until smooth and melted. Finally when the macaroni is done add it in the cheese mixture and stir it for a even coat.
